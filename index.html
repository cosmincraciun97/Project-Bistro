Our Story

Bistro is one of Romania's most beloved restaurants.It has welcomed guests to enjoy its contemporary Romanian cuisine, warm hospitality, and unparalleled service in Bucharest for over two decades. Our menu reflects Scarlatescu’s journey across the world as a chef. Also we have a dining concept that offers seasonal dishes with exotic flavors, utilising where possible, local suppliers. After 16 years in the restaurant business, 9 in farming, and several years dedicated to the study of nutrition, we decided now was the right time to take all that experience and synthesize it into a local eatery where people can enjoy exquisite food, support local food producers, and eat the kind of food they can feel good about.
